Spent a weekend moving everything around. It sounds like a small thing, but where I work and where I rest ended up shaping my days more than I expected — better light, less clutter, a clearer line between heads-down time and switching off.

My focus went up. So did my mood. I'm convinced now that environment is a feature, not a backdrop.
